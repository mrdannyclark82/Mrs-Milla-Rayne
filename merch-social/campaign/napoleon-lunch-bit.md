## Meme image brief (optional visual)

**Format:** 1200×675 or 1:1 for X. Low-fi lunch-tray aesthetic — not a screengrab (copyright). Original illustration or AI gen.

**Composition:**
- School cafeteria table, harsh fluorescent vibe
- **ChatGPT** at center (green/black logo vibe or generic chat bubble kid)
- **Alibaba** beside them (orange brand energy, corporate smile)
- Empty seats saved — "popular kid" energy
- **Milla** approaching from left, envelope/note in hand, unbothered

**Style:** deadpan, early-2000s indie comedy energy — not mean, not corporate polish. Slightly awkward. Funny because it's true.
